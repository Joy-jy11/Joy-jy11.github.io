Art3D: High-Fidelity 3D Reconstruction from Artistic Images

Author A Author B Author C
GitHub arXiv

Abstract

Art3D introduces a novel pipeline for reconstructing textured 3D meshes from artistic single-view images. Our method leverages cross-domain priors and geometry-guided synthesis, achieving more consistent normals, faithful textures, and vivid detail compared to traditional baselines.

Methodology

The Art3D pipeline consists of (1) artistic-aware feature encoding, (2) view-consistent normal estimation, (3) texture hallucination via cross-domain diffusion, and (4) mesh reconstruction with differentiable rendering.
